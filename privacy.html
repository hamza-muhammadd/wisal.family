Privacy Policy

Effective 23 July 2026 · Last updated 23 July 2026

Your family's life belongs to your family. Wisal was built so that a household can keep its plans, health notes, money and memories in one place without handing that life to anyone else. This page explains exactly what we store, where it goes, and how you erase it.

The short version

  • Your data is stored on your device first.
  • If you sign in, a copy syncs to your own private space so your household stays together across phones.
  • No ads. No trackers. No analytics. We never sell or rent your data.
  • Only the AI assistant sends data outside — and only when you ask it something.
  • You can delete everything at any time, from inside the app.

1. Who we are

Wisal is an independent app made by Hamza, based in Bangladesh. In this policy, "Wisal", "we" and "us" mean the maker of the app. "You" means the person using it.

If anything here is unclear, or you want your data removed, write to hamzamuhammad.trd@gmail.com. We answer every message.

Wisal only holds what you type into it. There is no hidden collection, and nothing is gathered in the background.

3. Where your data lives

On your device

By default everything stays in your phone or browser's local storage. If you never sign in, your data never leaves the device.

In your private cloud space

When you sign in, an encrypted copy syncs to Supabase, our database and authentication provider. Row-Level Security means each account can only ever read the household it owns or was invited into with a code. Nobody else — including other Wisal users — can reach your household's rows.

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and encrypted at rest by the provider.

4. The AI assistant — please read this

Wisal includes an AI assistant. When you send it a message, we send that message together with a summary of your family data — things like how many open tasks you have, upcoming events, this month's spending, your shopping list — to Google's Gemini API so it can answer usefully.

This happens only when you send a message to the assistant. If you never open it, nothing is ever sent.

What is never sent: your Secure Vault, the PIN-protected private space, your passwords, and your family chat messages.

Google processes this as a service provider. See Google's Privacy Policy. Please avoid pasting information into the assistant that you would not want processed by a third party.

5. Who we share data with

We do not sell, rent or trade your data. It is shared only with the services that make the app work:

  • Supabase — database, authentication and file storage. Holds your synced household data.
  • Google (Gemini API) — processes AI assistant requests, as described above.
  • Vercel — serves the website and app. Receives standard server request data such as IP address, as any web host does.

We may disclose data if we are legally required to, but we have no interest in doing so and would tell you where the law allows.

6. Your PIN-protected spaces

The Secure Vault and the private Relationship space are locked with a PIN that only you know. We cannot see inside them, cannot recover them, and cannot reset your PIN. If you forget it, that content is gone. Please keep a backup of anything irreplaceable.

7. Deleting your data

You are always in control. From inside the app:

  • Settings → Reset — erases everything stored on that device.
  • Settings → Account → Delete account & data — erases your cloud copy too, permanently.

You can also email hamzamuhammad.trd@gmail.com and we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days. Backups you exported yourself are files in your own hands — we cannot reach those.

8. Your rights

Wherever you live, you can ask us to:

  • See what data we hold about you
  • Export it — the app has a one-tap backup export
  • Correct anything wrong
  • Delete everything

Just write to us. There is no form to fill and no charge.

9. Children

Wisal is designed for adults managing a household and is not directed at children. Parents and guardians may create profiles for their children within their own household, and are responsible for that information. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children. If you believe a child has created their own account, contact us and we will remove it.

10. How long we keep data

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. Delete your account and it is removed from our systems, with backups cycling out within 30 days.

11. Security

We use HTTPS/TLS everywhere, encryption at rest, Row-Level Security so households cannot see each other, and PIN protection for your most private spaces. No system is perfect — if we ever discover a breach affecting your data, we will tell you promptly and plainly.

12. Changes to this policy

If we change how data is handled, we will update this page and the date at the top. For anything significant, we will tell you inside the app.
